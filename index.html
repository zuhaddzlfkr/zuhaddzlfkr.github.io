Perempuan Nusantara Monitoring Dashboard

Perempuan Nusantara is a women's community located around the PT. Japfa Comfeed Indonesia business units, empowered by the Social Investment Department PT. Japfa Comfeed Indonesia. Currently, five business unit regions have active women's communities that transform waste into soap and eco-enzyme, with a particular focus on producing eco-enzymes every month.

This dashboard monitors the weight of organic waste processed each month by each region and by individual members or groups within the Perempuan Nusantara community.

Personal Project

Digital Signal Conditioning: 2nd-Order Butterworth Filter Design and Performance Analysis

Implemented a digital low-pass filter using a 2nd-order IIR Butterworth architecture. A sample ambient audio with a chirp signal (10 kHz – 24 kHz) was used to simulate wideband noise interference.

Butterworth architecture was selected to maximise its naturally flat passband, ensuring the signal of interest remains undistorted. A digital implementation was chosen for design flexibility and scalability through rigorous parameter tuning and iteration.

Validated via Signal-to-Noise Ratio (SNR) analysis: achieved a 13.35 dB improvement, transitioning from −7.976 dB to +5.372 dB.

Architecture parameters: fc 5,000 Hz · fs 48,000 Hz

Key Result
13.35 dB SNR improvement — from −7.976 dB to +5.372 dB
